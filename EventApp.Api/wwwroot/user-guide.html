Summer Fest

Mobile Web App User Guide

Use the Summer Fest web application from your iPhone or Android phone to view the schedule, register for activities, check event information, and manage your account.

Best experience

Open the app in your phone browser and add it to your Home Screen. It will launch like a regular mobile app.

iPhone

  1. 1 Open SafariGo to the Summer Fest web app link.
  2. 2 Add to Home ScreenTap Share, then choose Add to Home Screen.
  3. 3 Launch the appOpen Summer Fest from your Home Screen and sign in.

Android

  1. 1 Open ChromeGo to the Summer Fest web app link.
  2. 2 Install the appTap the menu, then choose Install app or Add to Home screen.
  3. 3 Use from Home ScreenOpen Summer Fest and allow notifications if you want event reminders.

How To Use The App

Sign in or create an account

Use your personal details to sign in. If you do not have an account yet, switch to registration and complete the form.

View the schedule

Open Schedule to browse activities by date. Tap an activity to see time, place, description, availability, and participants.

Register for activities

Tap Register on an activity you want to join. If you change your plans, tap Cancel to remove your registration.

Check event information

Open Info for general event details, announcements, and useful notes from the organizers.

Manage your profile

Open Account to review your profile, update your details, and see the activities you registered for.

Tips

  • Keep the app updated by reopening it when the organizer announces changes.
  • Allow notifications to receive event reminders on supported browsers.
  • Use the bottom navigation on your phone to move between Schedule, Info, and Account.
  • If something does not load, refresh the page and check your internet connection.
